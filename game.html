Instructions

Objective

This is a two player game where you collect your ball and throw it at your opponent. Hit them before they hit you, and you get a point. First to 5 wins!

Controls

Player 1

A: Move left
D: Move right
W: Jump (when standing on something) or use jetpack
S: Look down
Space: Throw ball


Player 2

A: Move left
D: Move right
W: Jump (when standing on something) or use jetpack
S: Look down
Space: Throw ball
